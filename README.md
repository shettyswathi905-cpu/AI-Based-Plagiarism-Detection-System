## Overview

PlagiGuard XAI is an advanced AI-powered plagiarism detection and AI content verification platform designed to identify copied, paraphrased, translated, and AI-generated content. The system utilizes Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), OCR, Speech-to-Text, and Large Language Models (LLMs) to provide accurate and reliable plagiarism analysis across multiple content formats and languages.

---

## Problem Statement

Existing plagiarism detection systems mainly rely on keyword matching and exact text comparison. They often fail to detect paraphrased, translated, semantic, and AI-generated content. Most systems also lack support for multilingual analysis, image plagiarism detection, audio plagiarism detection, and explainable reporting, reducing their overall effectiveness and accuracy.

---

## Proposed Solution

PlagiGuard XAI is an AI-powered plagiarism detection system that uses Deep Learning, OCR, Speech-to-Text, and Large Language Models to detect plagiarism and AI-generated content from documents, images, audio files, and text. It supports multiple languages, provides accurate plagiarism analysis, detailed reports, and suggestions to improve content originality using semantic similarity and AI-based verification.

---

## Objectives

- Detect plagiarism in documents, images, audio, and text.
- Identify AI-generated content.
- Support more than 20 languages.
- Generate detailed plagiarism reports.
- Provide semantic similarity analysis.
- Extract text using OCR technology.
- Convert audio into text using Speech-to-Text.
- Generate explainable AI reports.
- Improve academic integrity and content originality.

## Results

The proposed hybrid AI model combines Sentence-BERT, RoBERTa, XLM-RoBERTa, OCR, Whisper, and Groq LLM verification to achieve high plagiarism detection accuracy. The system effectively identifies semantic plagiarism, AI-generated content, multilingual similarities, and multimodal plagiarism across text, image, and audio formats.
